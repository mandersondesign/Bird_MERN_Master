At Fleet Feet PNW, we want to inspire the athlete in everyone. By training with us, racing with us, and shopping with us, you help to bring our mission alive. We are excited that you have registered to train with us!

To get started:

• Download the Bird app using the links at the bottom of this email. You'll receive your workouts and can start conversations with your coach, all in the app!

• Keep an eye on your email inbox. The week that training begins, we'll send you an email with information about what to expect, where we meet for workouts, and other tips and tricks to maximize the training you do with us.

• If you have questions before training starts, please email us! Send a note to wade@fleetfeetpnw.com, and he'll either answer your question or connect you with your run leader.
